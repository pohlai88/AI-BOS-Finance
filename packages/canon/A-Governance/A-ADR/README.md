## ðŸ“– What is an ADR?

An **Architecture Decision Record (ADR)** is an immutable snapshot of a technical choice:

- **Context:** Why the decision was needed
- **Decision:** What was chosen
- **Consequences:** Positive and negative outcomes

ADRs are **never modified** after acceptance. If the decision changes, a new ADR supersedes the old one.
